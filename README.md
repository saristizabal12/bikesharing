# bikesharing
Creating a business proposal presentation for a bike sharing company: import data into Tableau, create style worksheets

Link to Tableau is: https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/sebastian.aristizabal/viz/NYCCitiBikeStory_16625921069750/NYCBikesharingStory?publish=yes

**Overview of the analysis:** Explain the purpose of this analysis.
The purpose of this project was to create an analysis of the CitiBike data in New York to present to an angel investor. Des Moines, Iowa has very similar characteristics to New York City, so we used their data in order to pitch the BikeShare similar growth potential.

**Summary:** Provide a high-level summary of the results and two additional visualizations that you would perform with the given dataset.
To conclude, bikesharing was successful mainly in the Manhattan area because of the amount of tourists. Brooklyn also had a good amount of traffic but in the center of Manhattan where there are more attractions had a significant amount of traffic.
- Most Males road bikes and had a longer trip times than Females. 
- Most of the trips were around the weekend and it was more popular in mornings and evenings. Most subscribers had heavy traffic on Thursday and Friday meanwhile unsubcribed customers preferred the weekend.
